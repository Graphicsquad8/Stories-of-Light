📌 Trending Content Section Update

There will be 5 total contents (Trending Five)
Only 3 contents will be visible at a time on the dashboard
The remaining 2 contents will be accessible via an icon/slider button
Clicking the button will slide in the remaining content from right to left

When clicking this button, the following 4 filter options should appear:
Article
Motivational
Duas
Books
Based on the selected option:
Show 3 contents from the Top 5 trending items of that category
The currently selected category:
Should be displayed as an icon/button on the left side of the “View All” button
This should update dynamically when another category is selected

Name
Join date

Join Date
Number of Articles/Stories/Books published
Views received by category
Overall performance data
